# Learning Journey

> Documenting my transition from Backend Engineering to Cloud AI Architecture through structured learning, practical implementation, and continuous experimentation.

---

# Why AI?

For more than 14 years, I have been building backend systems, cloud-native applications, and distributed microservices.

While software systems have become increasingly scalable and resilient, the next generation of applications is becoming intelligent.

I started this journey to understand not only how AI models work but also how to design production systems that effectively integrate them.

My objective is to bridge traditional software engineering with modern AI engineering.

---

# Why the IBM AI Engineering Professional Certificate?

I wanted a structured program that covered the complete AI engineering lifecycle rather than isolated topics.

The IBM AI Engineering Professional Certificate provided a progression through:

- Machine Learning
- Deep Learning
- TensorFlow
- PyTorch
- Generative AI
- Large Language Models

It became the foundation for building a broader AI engineering knowledge base.

## Build

## Explain

# Lessons Learned

Some principles have become clear throughout this journey:

- Build before optimizing.
- Focus on concepts before frameworks.
- Understand architectures instead of memorizing APIs.
- Production AI requires software engineering discipline.
- Learning is more effective when accompanied by documentation.
