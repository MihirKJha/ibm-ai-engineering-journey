# AI Engineering Notes

> A structured collection of production-focused engineering notes covering Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI Agents, and Enterprise AI Systems.

These notes complement the hands-on labs and projects from the IBM AI Engineering Professional Certificate while extending beyond the course to include production concepts, architecture patterns, engineering best practices, and real-world AI system design.

---

# Purpose

The goal of these notes is to build a long-term AI engineering knowledge base that focuses on understanding concepts rather than simply completing tutorials.

Each note is designed to bridge the gap between:

- AI theory
- Hands-on implementation
- Production engineering
- Enterprise AI architecture

Rather than documenting APIs alone, these notes explain **why** technologies work, **how** they are used in practice, and **where** they fit into modern AI systems.

# Learning Philosophy

These notes are written from an engineering perspective.

The focus is on answering questions such as:

- Why does this concept exist?
- What problem does it solve?
- How does it work internally?
- Where is it used in production?
- What are the common trade-offs?
- How does it connect to other AI components?

The emphasis is on understanding systems rather than memorizing APIs.
